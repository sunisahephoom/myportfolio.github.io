Braised lamb shank

My brownie recipe

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour - 33 grams

  • Cacao power (unsweetened) - 20 grams

  • Butter - 45 grams

  • Oil - 7 grams

  • White granulate sugar - 30 grams

  • Brown sugar - 30 grams

  • Chocolate chips - 30 grams

  • Egg - 1

  • Vanila essence - 1 tsp

  • A pinch of salt

    • Instruction

    1. Preheat the oven to 175 C

    2. Melt the butter

    3. Mix butter with oil

    4. In a seperate bowl, beat the eggs with sugar

    5. slowly add the butter + oil into the egg mixture

    6. Once it is well combined, add salt and vanila essence

    7. Then, pour down the flour and cacao power by using the siff

    8. Mix everything together
